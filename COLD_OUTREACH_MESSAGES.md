## 🎯 Message Strategy

**Core Principles:**
1. **Lead with value, not pitch** — Solve their problem in the DM itself
2. **Specificity over generics** — Use real numbers, concrete examples
3. **No fluff** — Every word earns its place
4. **Clear CTA** — One specific next step

**Tone**: Direct, helpful, founder-to-founder. No corporate jargon.

## 🎯 Personalization Checklist (Use Before Sending)

Before hitting send, replace these placeholders:

- [ ] **[Name]** — Use first name only (casual, not "Mr./Ms.")
- [ ] **[Company]** — Include if relevant; omit if too formal
- [ ] **[specific observation]** — CRITICAL. Mention a recent hire, funding round, tweet, or product launch
- [ ] **[specific use case]** — Tailor to their industry (e.g., "SaaS lead scoring" vs "agency client enrichment")
- [ ] **Check their timezone** — Send during business hours (9am-4pm local time)

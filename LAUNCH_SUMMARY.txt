## 💡 Key Insights from This Work

### Landing Page:
- **Current strength**: Clean design, clear service breakdown, visible demos
- **Main weakness**: No urgency/scarcity, hero is too generic
- **Quick win**: Social proof + specificity = 20-30% conversion lift (industry avg)

### Outreach:
- **Best approach**: Value-first (Template 3: Free script) for cold contacts
- **Best closer**: ROI Calculator (Template 4) for warm leads
- **Avoid**: Generic "checking in" messages — they get ignored

- **$199 Beta pricing** is aggressive (competitors charge $1k-5k for similar)
- **"You own the workflow"** positioning differentiates from SaaS subscriptions

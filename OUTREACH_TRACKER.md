## 🎯 Outreach Strategy (Platform-Specific)

### LinkedIn Strategy
**Daily Limit**: 10 DMs/day (avoid spam flags)  
**Best Time**: 9-11 AM Tuesday-Thursday (highest open rates)  
**Personalization Required**: Mention recent post/activity in first line  
**Template Priority**: #1 (Lead Enrichment) or #2 (Meeting Chaos)

**Script**:
1. View their profile (creates notification)
2. Wait 1-2 hours
3. Send personalized DM
4. If no reply in 3 days → soft bump
5. If no reply in 7 days → move to nurture

---

### Email Strategy
**Daily Limit**: 25/day (Gmail sending limits)  
**Best Time**: 10 AM - 2 PM local time, avoid Mondays  
**Subject Line A/B Test**:
- "Save 6+ hours/week on lead research" (Template 1)
- "Stop losing decisions in meeting notes" (Template 2)
- "What's 10 hours/week worth to you?" (Template 4)

**Follow-Up Sequence**:
- Day 0: Initial email
- Day 3: Bump (if opened, no reply)
- Day 7: Value-add close + free resource
- Day 14+: Move to nurture list

---

### Twitter/X Strategy
**Daily Limit**: 15 DMs/day  
**Best Approach**: Reply to their tweet first, then DM  
**Template Priority**: #3 (Free Value First) or #5 (Pattern Interrupt)

**Script**:
1. Find recent tweet about pain point (lead gen, meetings, automation)
2. Reply with genuine insight or helpful resource
3. Wait 2-4 hours
4. Send DM with free value (script/template)
5. Mention beta offer as P.S.

## 🔥 Conversion Playbook

### When They Reply "Interested"
**Response Time**: <2 hours (strike while hot)

**Script**:
```
Awesome! Two quick questions to make sure this fits:
1. How much time does your team spend on [specific pain point] weekly?
2. What tools do you currently use?

I'll send a demo + setup timeline based on your answers.

When works for a 15-min call? [Calendar link or specific times]
```

**Next Step**: Book call → Send demo → Close sale

---

### When They Reply "Too Busy"
**Response Time**: Same day

**Script**:
```
Totally get it. Want me to ping you in 2 weeks?

Or I can send the setup doc now and you can revisit when timing's better. Either way works.
```

**Next Step**: Add to calendar for follow-up

---

### When They Ghost
**Day 3 Bump**:
```
Quick follow-up — did the demo above make sense for your workflow?

If timing's bad, no worries. If it's interesting, I can send setup docs today.
```

**Day 7 Final Touch**:
```
Last ping — closing beta slots this week. $199 rate ends Friday.

If not a fit, no problem. Here's a free resource anyway: [link]
```

**After Day 7**: Move to nurture, don't burn bridge
